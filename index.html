Brewery Site Prediction with Data
My Data Incubator Capstone uses machine learning to identify sites for opening new breweries in the United States at the county level.
The US Microbrewing industry is booming, with no signs of slowing down, and so the prospect of getting into the sector is only becoming more alluring. Choosing the site for any business is daunting. Choosing the right location is as important as securing funding and having industry know-how. I was curious about how I would choose a site for a brewery, and so I decided to find a way to use data to help make that decision.
üç∫
My algorithm uses demographic information provided by the US Census Bureau, Brewery location information from the Open Brewery Database, and brewery founding dates kindly provided by the Brewers Association.
The algorithm outputs a prediction of whether at least one new brewery will open in a given county in the next year. The results are summarized in the map above with lower probabilities in blue, and the highest probabilities are red.
